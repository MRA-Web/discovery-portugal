Discover our services

Get your NIF Portugal Online with us

Discovery is a digital solution that simplifies the process of obtaining the Tax Identification Number (NIF) for residents and non-residents in Portugal. With this service, you can request your NIF quickly and securely, without the need to travel to public offices. Simply fill out an online form and submit the necessary documents, and you will soon receive your NIF by email. Simplify your life in Portugal with Discovery Portugal!

Get your NISS Portugal Online with us

NISS Portugal Online is a convenient service that makes it easy to obtain a Social Security Identification Number (NISS) in Portugal. With this online service, residents and non-residents of Portugal can request their NISS without complications. Simply fill out an online form and submit the necessary documents, and you will soon receive your NISS by email.
