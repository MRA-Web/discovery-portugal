Discover our services

Secure your Portuguese NIF online with us!

Discovery offers a digital solution that streamlines the process of obtaining the Tax Identification Number (NIF) for both residents and non-residents in Portugal. Our service allows you to request your NIF quickly and securely, eliminating the need to visit public offices. Simply complete an online form and submit the required documents. Your NIF will be delivered to you via email shortly after. Simplify your life in Portugal with Discovery Portugal!

Get your NISS Portugal Online with us!

NISS Portugal Online is a convenient service that simplifies the process of obtaining a Social Security Identification Number (NISS) in Portugal. With this online service, residents and non-residents in Portugal can request their NISS hassle-free. Simply complete an online form and submit the necessary documents, and you will soon receive your NISS by email.
